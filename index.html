Welcome to Arkan


An immersive third and first-person shooter game where you play as an Arkan hero fighting off waves of mutated monsters sent by a mysterious dark force to eradicate the population of Arkan.


Your objective is to buy time for civilians and resources to be extracted to safe zones as the world of Arkan falls.

With various guns at your disposal, play solo or team up with others in co-op mode to survive as long as possible and get as many kills as you can.


Arkan offers an intense and challenging environment for players who enjoy cooperative third-person and first-person shooters. Join forces with your friends and become an Arkan hero to save your world from destruction.
